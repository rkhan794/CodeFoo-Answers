The Northman Review

The Northman hits theaters on April 22, 2022. Review by Siddhant Adlakha.
Robert Eggers' viking revenge saga The Northman works best when it dives head-first into
dreams and disorienting visions, but it slows down when it becomes a more traditional
Hollywood narrative. With viciousness relegated to its margins, it often feels neutered and
bloodless, but still ends up on the right side of entertaining thanks to its pulsating music
and measured performances.
